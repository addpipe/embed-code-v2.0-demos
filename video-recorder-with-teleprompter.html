Video Recorder With Teleprompter

Made by the Pipe Video Recording Platform

This demo page embeds the Pipe Video Recording Client and a teleprompter. The two are linked through the recorder's JS Events API. When you start recording, the teleprompter also starts.

You can study the source code on GitHub.
